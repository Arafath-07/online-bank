Online Bank

An online bank is a financial institution that operates primarily through digital platforms without a physical branch network. Customers can manage their accounts, transfer funds, pay bills, and access a wide range of financial services entirely online or via mobile apps. Online banks often offer lower fees, higher interest rates on savings, and convenient, 24/7 access to banking services. Examples include Ally Bank, Chime, and Revolut

Variety Of Payment Choices

A variety of payment choices allows customers to choose from multiple payment options like credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, online platforms (PayPal), bank transfers, and even cryptocurrency. Offering diverse payment methods enhances convenience, reduces cart abandonment, and caters to a broader range of customer preferences
